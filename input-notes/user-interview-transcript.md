## Participant

Employee user in Hamburg. Activated membership two weeks ago.

## Transcript

Interviewer: Tell me about the first time you used FitPass Demo.

Participant: I remember activating the membership. That part worked, but after that I was not really sure what to do next.

Interviewer: What did you try?

Participant: I searched near my office because I wanted something after work. I saw a few gyms, but I had to open each one to check whether they had evening classes.

Interviewer: Did you book anything?

Participant: Not the first time. I came back later and booked a class, but I wanted a clearer confirmation. I was not sure if it was definitely booked.

Interviewer: What would have made it easier?

Participant: After activation, I would have liked one clear next step. Maybe show me three nearby options that match my schedule.

Interviewer: Anything else?

Participant: If the app helped me remember what I did each week, I might use it more often. Right now I forget about it unless someone mentions it.
