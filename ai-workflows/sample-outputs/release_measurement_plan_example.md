# Release Measurement Plan

This is the human readable companion to `ai_release_measurement_plan.json`. It is a fictional mock AI output for review.

## Release Candidates

| Initiative | Audience | Metric | Window | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Improve check in fallback | Employees, support, customer success, partner support | Check In Failed Count | Two weeks after release | Product Ops |
| Launch activation reminder kit | Employees, customer success, employer administrators | Membership Activation Rate | Four weeks after release | Customer Success |

## Communication Notes

- Improve check in fallback should explain what users and support teams do when a gym check in fails.
- Launch activation reminder kit should explain how eligible employees activate and find a useful first activity.

## Measurement Notes

- Check failed check ins and support contacts after the fallback release.
- Check activation rate and support contacts after the reminder kit release.
- If the metric does not move, review whether the issue is product flow, partner operations, employer communication, or data quality.

## Review Questions

1. Which release candidate has enough evidence to move forward?
2. Which internal teams need enablement before launch?
3. Which metric will prove whether the release helped?
