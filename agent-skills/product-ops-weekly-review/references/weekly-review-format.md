# Weekly Product Ops Review Format

Use this format for a concise weekly Product Ops readout. It matches the output of
`ai-workflows/prompts/weekly_product_insights.md`.

## Template

```text
# Weekly Product Insights

## What Changed
- ...

## Product Readout
| Area | Signal | Suggested Follow-Up |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ... | ... | ... |

## Decision Questions
1. ...
2. ...
```

## Audience

Write for product, engineering, support, customer success, sales, marketing, and operations.

## Tone

- Practical and specific
- Short enough to scan
- Clear about uncertainty
- Focused on decisions and follow-up
